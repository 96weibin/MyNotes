## Quick Use Guide
- Team Updates: keep each team update short and focused on progress, risks, and dependencies.
- Cross-Team Dependencies: only capture dependencies that affect schedule, testing, or delivery confidence.
- Risks / Escalations: record items that may impact multiple teams or key milestones.
- Parking Lot: move detailed technical discussion out of the weekly sync.
- Follow-up Actions: every important issue should end with a clear owner and due date.
