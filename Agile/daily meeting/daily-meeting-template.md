## Quick Use Guide
- Completed / Closed: only items that are truly done or no longer need tracking.
- On Track: items moving normally with no major concern.
- Risks / Concerns: items that may slip, depend on others, or remain unclear.
- Follow-up Needed: specific actions after the meeting. This is the most important section.
- Parking Lot: topics that should not be discussed in detail during the daily.
